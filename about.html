We are The Magic Pages

The project entitled “The Magic Pages” The main objective of the project is to create an online book store that allows users to search and purchase a book online based on title, author and subject. Using this Website the user can purchase a book online instead of going out to a book store and wasting time. Many people choose to conduct shopping online because of the convenience. For example, when a person shops at a brick-and-mortar store, He/she has to drive to the store, find a parking place, and walk throughout the store until He/ she locates the books He/she needs. After finding the items she wants to purchase, He/she may often need to stand in long lines at the cash register.

As the craze of reading of books among peoples after the period of covid-19 is increasing day by day . So Building an online book selling website provides an alternative means of ecommerce for selling school books and vulnerable books for the students and remaining people. The system also comes with a great benefit which is that it also provides an enquiry of books and also the uploading and purchasing book with a single click on the button.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality but affordable books for education, entertainment, self-development and self-fulfillment. Providing a wide range of books to satisfy our clients

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the largest and leading old book distributor in India and to be the bookselling household name.
